OUR OBJECTIVE.

To catalyze the innovation needed to leverage geothermal power to reduce post-harvest loss by developing a new market for dried vegetables.

Why dried vegetables?

Nutritious and long-lasting.

Easy to store and transport.

Other food products can be developed from dried vegetables.

Levels of innovation.

Innovation in drying technologies which can be achieved through solar dryers, electric dryers, biogas driven dryers.

Innovation in food science with immense range of foods that can be made. The key is creativity of the food technologists.

Innovation in marketing to introduce dried products to consumers. One way to increase consumption is to use cookery competitions and using celebrity chefs to showcase dried vegetables.
